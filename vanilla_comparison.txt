	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	|                                             NOTES                                             |
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| DIKTAT, HEGEMONY, and REVOLUTIONARY names are too specific to lore to comfortably expand upon.|
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| FRENCH, GERMAN, RUSSIAN, and SPANISH names are exclusive to MSN.                              |
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| Factions choose ship names from multiple categories.                                          |
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| Although the HEGEMONY list category has only 26 names,                                        |
	| the Hegemony faction will additionally pull names from GENERAL and BRITISH lists.             |
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| The above is true for all other factions.                                                     |
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| Mods that include references to vanilla naming categories will automatically use names from   |
	| the expanded naming lists (and weighting values) of MSN.                                      |
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| Delete the *.faction files in the data/world/factions folder to revert to the vanilla         |
	| "shipNameSources" random name selection weighting values.                                     |
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| Edit the "gameVersion": value in mod_info.json to make MSN work with different game versions. |
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| In the vanilla faction files, the PLAYER faction only used the SPACE category.                |
	+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
